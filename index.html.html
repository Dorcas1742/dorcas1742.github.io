Undergraduate Research

Improvement on the Efficiency of Cuttings and Marcotting Propagation Techniques of Kolanut (Cola acuminata) Using Organic Supplements

This research investigated the potential of locally available organic supplements to improve the rooting efficiency of vegetative propagation techniques in Kolanut — an economically and culturally significant tree species. The study generated evidence for low-cost, indigenous-knowledge-driven approaches to tree propagation in sustainable agriculture and agroforestry.

Organic supplements tested:

Aloe veraOnionGarlic Banana peelMoringaGinger
